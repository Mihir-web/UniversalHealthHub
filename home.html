FOOT CARE

Complete & true care can only be provided when it is convenient. That's true in the case of Foot care as well. That's why we provide Foot Care wherever you are. Your retirement home & community center, Long Term Care center or even the comfort of your home.

Book now

Diabetic foot care is a kind of care that needs professional knowledge & expertise. And that's exactly what our nurses provide. Along with a lot of compassion of course. Our registered nursing professionals offer personalized diabetic foot care in your home, community, and clinics & make sure you have happy & healthy feet.

Book now

We at UHH, think beyond just being the best foot care provider. For us, it's all about serving a community. Think of us like an extended family member, who wants to educate & make the community aware about foot care. That's why we partner with Long Term Care homes, Retirement homes & more & set up a mobile clinic to provide tailored foot care to one and all.

NURSE EDUCATION

We believe educating is as vital as serving. Our community includes those we care for and those we educate. Our certificate program in Basic and Advanced Diabetic Nursing Foot Care with Basic Wound Care (approved for quality by the Foot Care Nurses Association of Toronto and GTA.) is designed for both caregivers and care receivers.

Book now

We also provide on-site training for Personal Support Workers and nurses at LTC homes and retirement facilities, ensuring everyone receives the best care possible. We train them in Dementia Care, Foot & Wound care & also sharpen their skills to become technology friendly.
